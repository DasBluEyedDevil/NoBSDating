# Privacy Policy

**Last Updated: November 14, 2025**

NoBS Dating ("we," "our," or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application and services (the "Service").

## 1. Information We Collect

### 1.1 Information You Provide
**Account Information:**
- Authentication data (from Apple Sign-In or Google Sign-In)
- Email address
- Name
- Date of birth
- Gender and gender preferences

**Profile Information:**
- Photos
- Bio and personal description
- Interests and preferences

**User Content:**
- Messages sent through the Service
- Reports and feedback submitted

### 1.2 Information Collected Automatically
**Usage Data:**
- App interactions and features used
- Matches, likes, and passes
- Time spent on the app
- Subscription and purchase history

**Device Information:**
- Device model and operating system
- Unique device identifiers
- Mobile network information
- IP address

**Location Data:**
- Approximate location (when permission granted)
- Used for distance calculations and local matches
- Never shared with other users in exact form (only approximate distance shown)

### 1.3 Information from Third Parties
- OAuth provider data (Apple, Google)
- Payment information from app stores (Apple App Store, Google Play)

## 2. How We Use Your Information

We use your information to:

### 2.1 Provide the Service
- Create and maintain your account
- Match you with other users based on preferences
- Enable communication between matched users
- Process subscriptions and payments

### 2.2 Improve the Service
- Analyze usage patterns and trends
- Develop new features
- Optimize performance and user experience
- Conduct research and analytics

### 2.3 Safety and Security
- Verify identity and prevent fraud
- Enforce our Terms of Service
- Respond to legal requests
- Detect and prevent abuse, harassment, or illegal activity

### 2.4 Communications
- Send service-related notifications
- Provide customer support
- Send promotional messages (with your consent)
- Notify you of matches and messages

## 3. How We Share Your Information

### 3.1 With Other Users
- Profile information (name, age, bio, photos, interests) is visible to users you match with
- Approximate distance (not exact location) is shown to potential matches
- Messages are shared only with your matched users

### 3.2 With Service Providers
We may share information with third-party providers who perform services on our behalf:
- Cloud hosting providers (for data storage)
- Analytics providers (Firebase, Sentry)
- Payment processors (Apple, Google)
- Customer support tools

### 3.3 For Legal Reasons
We may disclose information if required to:
- Comply with legal obligations
- Respond to lawful requests from authorities
- Protect our rights and property
- Investigate fraud or security issues
- Protect user safety

### 3.4 Business Transfers
If we are involved in a merger, acquisition, or sale of assets, your information may be transferred. You will be notified of any such change.

## 4. Data Retention

- **Active Accounts:** We retain your information while your account is active
- **Deleted Accounts:** Most information is deleted within 30 days of account deletion
- **Legal Requirements:** Some data may be retained longer to comply with legal obligations
- **Backups:** Data in backups may persist for up to 90 days

## 5. Your Rights and Choices

### 5.1 Access and Correction
- You can access and update your profile information in the app
- You can request a copy of your data by contacting support

### 5.2 Account Deletion
- You can delete your account at any time through app settings
- Deletion is permanent and cannot be undone
- Some information may remain in backups for up to 90 days

### 5.3 Location Services
- You can enable or disable location services in device settings
- Disabling location will limit matching to non-distance-based criteria

### 5.4 Marketing Communications
- You can opt out of promotional emails and notifications
- Service-related notifications cannot be disabled while account is active

### 5.5 Data Portability
- You can request an export of your data in a machine-readable format

## 6. Cookies and Tracking Technologies

We use:
- **Session tokens:** For authentication and security
- **Analytics:** To understand app usage (Firebase Analytics)
- **Crash reporting:** To identify and fix bugs (Sentry)

## 7. Third-Party Services

Our Service integrates with:
- **Apple Sign-In / Google Sign-In:** For authentication
- **Firebase:** For analytics and crash reporting
- **RevenueCat:** For subscription management
- **App Stores:** For payments and distribution

These services have their own privacy policies. We recommend reviewing them.

## 8. Children's Privacy

Our Service is not intended for users under 18. We do not knowingly collect information from children. If we discover we have collected information from a child, we will delete it immediately.

## 9. International Data Transfers

Your information may be transferred to and processed in countries other than your own. We ensure appropriate safeguards are in place to protect your information.

## 10. Data Security

We implement appropriate technical and organizational measures to protect your information:
- Encryption in transit (HTTPS/TLS)
- Encryption at rest for sensitive data
- Access controls and authentication
- Regular security audits

However, no method of transmission or storage is 100% secure. We cannot guarantee absolute security.

## 11. California Privacy Rights (CCPA)

If you are a California resident, you have the right to:
- Know what personal information we collect
- Know whether we sell or share personal information (we don't)
- Access your personal information
- Delete your personal information
- Opt-out of sale of personal information (not applicable)
- Non-discrimination for exercising your rights

## 12. European Privacy Rights (GDPR)

If you are in the European Economic Area, you have the right to:
- Access your personal data
- Rectify inaccurate data
- Erase your data ("right to be forgotten")
- Restrict processing
- Data portability
- Object to processing
- Lodge a complaint with a supervisory authority

## 13. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of material changes by:
- Posting the new Privacy Policy in the app
- Sending a notification
- Updating the "Last Updated" date

Continued use after changes constitutes acceptance.
